# About

omnipath-metabo is developed by the
[Saez-Rodriguez group](https://saezlab.org/) at Heidelberg University Hospital.

## Links

- **Source code**: [github.com/saezlab/omnipath-metabo](https://github.com/saezlab/omnipath-metabo)
- **Web service**: [metabo.omnipathdb.org](https://metabo.omnipathdb.org)
- **OmniPath**: [omnipathdb.org](https://omnipathdb.org/)
- **Issues**: [github.com/saezlab/omnipath-metabo/issues](https://github.com/saezlab/omnipath-metabo/issues)

## Data licensing

The data served by omnipath-metabo is combined from many original resources,
each with their own license terms. The omnipath-metabo software is
BSD-3-Clause, but the **data** is subject to the licenses of the original
sources. Some resources restrict commercial use.
